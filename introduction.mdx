Staminads is a platform for measuring the performance of your advertising campaigns by focusing on the user-engagement.

## Essential Concepts

**The 2 core pillars of Staminads are:**
1. Quantity: Volume of sessions
2. Quality: TimeScore

### TimeScore

Web marketers are used to measure the performance of their campaigns by looking at conversions (ROI, ROAS etc...). Sadly, **due to technical and legal constraints this is not possible anymore** (adblockers, user consent, cookie lifetime, cross-device journeys etc...).

Staminads brings a new way to measure the performance of your advertising campaigns by focusing on the user-engagement. And the best way to measure user-engagement is to look at the **time spent on your website**.

The **TimeScore** is the **median** value of the time spent on your website for each source of traffic over a given period of time, expressed in seconds.

### eTraffic

The **eTraffic** (effective traffic) is the volume of sessions weighted by the TimeScore, relative to the total volume of sessions and highest possible TimeScore.

The **eTraffic** is used to compare the performance of different sources of traffic & attribute conversions.
