Using Staminads is straightforward, you just need to create a workspace, copy & paste the JavaScript snippet on your website and you're good to go.

## Create a workspace

Start by entering your website URL. It will automatically detect your website name, timezone, currency and logo. Feel free to edit them to fit your needs in the following screen.
